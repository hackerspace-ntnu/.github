## Hackerspace NTNU's GitHub Organization

![Hackerspace Logo](./images/hackerspace.png)

Welcome!

[Hackerspace NTNU](https://hackerspace-ntnu.no) is a student-run project at [NTNU](https://www.ntnu.no) and is open to all students regardless of their field of study.
Here you will find new technology at your disposal, including 3D printers and virtual reality equipment.
Whether you want to make an LED lamp flash or create a 3D model of Trondheim, the members of Hackerspace can offer you a wide range of expertise and help you with your project, regardless of your membership in Hackerspace.

Feel free to take a look at our various projects! We have repositories for projects led by DevOps and various project groups.
